This is an alpha version of my portfolio:

- Many features have been removed
- Not optimised yet
- Press T to write whispers (visible by everyone)
- Desktop only

Controls

- WASD or ARROWS to move around
- SHIFT to boost
- B to brake
- SPACE to jump
- ENTER to interact
- T to post a whisper
- R to respawn
- NUMPAD to activate hydraulics

Future versions will have controller and touch support.
